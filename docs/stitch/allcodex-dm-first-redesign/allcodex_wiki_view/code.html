Biography

Born into the waning shadows of the Stonehaven Clan, Aldric was never meant for the throne. While his brothers practiced the broadsword in the courtyard of Crimson Keep, Aldric found himself navigating the intricate labyrinths of the royal archives. It was here, amidst the dust and parchment, that he discovered his true calling: the weight of secrets.

"A king rules by the sword, but a Spymaster rules by the silence between words. Aldric knows the silence better than any man alive." — Chronicler Vaelen, Year 432

The Iron Veil Era

The formation of the Order of the Iron Veil marked Aldric's ascension to true power. Tasked by Lord Maren to purge the internal rot of the Northern Campaign, Aldric utilized a network of informants stretching from the docks of Oakhaven to the peaks of the Frozen Spine.

His methods were often described as "spectral"—striking from the shadows with surgical precision and leaving behind only a signature seal of violet wax.

Current Status

As of the current timeline, Aldric resides in the high towers of the Crimson Keep. Though officially retired from active field work, his presence looms over every political negotiation in the kingdom.
