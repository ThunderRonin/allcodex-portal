Once the high-seer of the Obsidian Citadel, Aelarion sacrificed his mortality during the Great Conjunction of the Twin Suns. His flesh was woven with the threads of the Void, ensuring his spirit could never truly depart the realm of Aethelgard. For three millennia, he has sat upon the Frost-Throne, watching empires crumble into dust while his own power only grew in the suffocating silence of his tomb.
